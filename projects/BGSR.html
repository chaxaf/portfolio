Slip Ring Electronics Engineering Internship

Engineering internship at Beyond Gravity focusing on electronics design support, requirements and verification management, and industrialization of high-reliability slip ring products for space and defense applications.

Overview

During this internship, I worked within the slip ring electronics engineering team, contributing to product definition, documentation, verification, and manufacturing preparation for rotary transfer solutions used in demanding environments. The role bridged system requirements, detailed documentation, and practical assembly and test activities.

Responsibilities

  • Supported concept selection, technical definition, and documentation of electronics solutions.
  • Compiled and maintained the manufacturing data package (manufacturing file).
  • Defined and supported assembly and test processes, tools, and requirements.
  • Managed requirements and verification, including verification control and flow-down.
  • Handled requirement deviations, non-conformities, and updates (RfD, NC, NCF).
  • Maintained and issued the Verification Control Document (VCD).
  • Produced technical documentation such as Design Descriptions, User Manuals, procurement specifications, test plans, and test procedures.
  • Interfaced with customers and suppliers on technical topics and requirement status.
  • Provided technical support for proposals and contributed to R&D roadmap activities.

Engineering & Verification
