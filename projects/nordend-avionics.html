Nordend Launch Vehicle Avionics

As avionics team leader for the EPFL Rocket Team’s Nordend project, I led the design and implementation of the rocket’s flight computer, telemetry system, and supporting hardware.

Overview

Nordend is a bi-liquid launch vehicle developed to compete in the European rocketry Challenge EuRoc 2023 at 10 kft. The avionics system controlled the propulsion unit, collected flight data, and relayed telemetry to the ground segment.

Responsibilities

  • Led an 8-person avionics team from concept to flight-ready hardware.
  • Defined system architecture including custom mainboard, power and sensor modules.
  • Developed real-time firmware on a Cortex-M4 microcontroller for sensor fusion and event handling.
  • Coordinated development of GNSS, Wi-Fi and radio antennas with dedicated sub-teams.
